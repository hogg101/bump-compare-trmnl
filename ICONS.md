## Icon Specifications

- **Format**: 1-bit PNG
- **Size**: 256×256 pixels (or higher, will be scaled)
- **Style**: Bold silhouettes, high contrast, suitable for e-ink displays
- **Background**: Transparent (converted to 1-bit)
- **Naming**: Must match filename exactly (case-sensitive)
